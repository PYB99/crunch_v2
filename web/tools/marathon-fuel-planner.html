2:1Above 60 g/hr you need multiple carb sources (glucose + fructose, ~2:1) — a single glucose gel maxes out around 60 g/hr. Most "beta fuel / dual-carb" products already do this.
The science
Carb-load 10–12 g/kg, 36–48 h out — Burke LM et al., J Sports Sciences 2011; ACSM/AND Joint Position Statement 2016. Race-day 60–90 g/hr (up to 90–120 g/hr gut-trained) via multiple transportable carbs — Jeukendrup A, Sports Medicine 2011/2014. Taper & loading — Mujika & Padilla, MSSE 2003. This tool is general guidance, not medical or individual dietary advice — rehearse every strategy in training first.
